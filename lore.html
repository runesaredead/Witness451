COME, BEAR WITNESS

Witness 451 is a labor of love: conceived, generated, and hand-curated from a million potential deterministic art pieces.

From the demented mind of Rad.

"The eye is the window to the soul." This collection provides a glimpse into mine: chaotic, irreverent, audacious, perhaps a bit unhinged.

This is my debut Ordinals collection; the first step in a long road. I have backlogs of art, games, and half-mad ideas I've resolved to drag into the light, to share with you. I will never stop creating so long as I draw breath.
I wouldn't know how..

I make only one promise to holders of 451:
Whatever I do in this space, you will have first dibs.

You are my core audience. My believer. My witness.
You are who I do this for.

Thank you.

— Rad
